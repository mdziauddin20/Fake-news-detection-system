⚙️ How It Works

01 📝 Paste Article

Copy and paste the full news article or headline you want to verify into the text box above. Longer articles provide more data for accurate analysis.

02 🤖 AI Processing

TF-IDF vectorization extracts 10,000 linguistic features (unigrams + bigrams). Our ensemble model (PAC, LogReg, NaiveBayes) processes these features with soft voting.

03 ⚡ Instant Result

Get a credibility verdict with confidence score (70% threshold for certainty), full article statistics, and uncertainty alerts in milliseconds.
